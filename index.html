Payment Strategy — The Right Answer
💳 PAYSTACK — NIGERIA
Paystack for Nigerian guests. Supports ₦ Naira, bank transfer (NIP), USSD, OPay, GTBank, Access Bank, all local cards. No CBN restrictions. Settles to your Nigerian bank account.
🌍 STRIPE — DIASPORA
Stripe (Premium) for diaspora guests. Collect GBP, USD, CAD, EUR. Nigerian cards often fail on Stripe due to CBN — which is why Paystack handles Nigeria. Both = complete global coverage.

How Paystack Auto-Confirms
1
Guest pays on your event page — via card, bank transfer (NIP), or USSD. Paystack processes the payment in seconds.
2
Paystack sends a webhook — an automatic signal to AsoEbi Flow the instant the payment succeeds. This is a secure, industry-standard mechanism.
3
AsoEbi Flow receives it — our server verifies the payment signature, confirms it's legitimate, and marks the booking as "Confirmed".
4
Your dashboard updates instantly — the order moves from Pending to Confirmed. Revenue KPIs update in real time. You do nothing.
5
Paystack settles your funds — money is transferred to your linked bank account on Paystack's settlement schedule (typically next business day).
